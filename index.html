Education Centres in Seville Compatible with the CV

This selection includes schools and language centres in Seville and its metropolitan area that match a profile as an English teacher with Level 5 TEFL certification, secondary education experience, bilingual or international education, IB curriculum exposure, and intercultural competence. The list has been expanded to include all state-subsidised (concertado) and private schools offering primary and secondary education in the metropolitan area, excluding marginal neighbourhoods.

Compatibility criterion: priority has been given to international, bilingual, and IB-oriented schools, as well as English academies with native or bilingual teachers and programmes for teenagers, adults, schools, or companies. New additions include concertado and private schools across the Aljarafe, Los Alcores, Dos Hermanas, Utrera, and central Seville.
